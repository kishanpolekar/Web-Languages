Ordered lists

They use numbers to form outlines.
(The kind of numeral is controlled using CSS.)

  1. To be
  2. Or not to be

I can tell it what number to start on:

  1. School
  2. Unemployment
  3. Job

You can force an order with specific values:

  1. Painting in a gallery
  2. Painting in a gallery
  3. Painting in a gallery

Lists can be multilayered:

  1. Friends
  2. Family
    1. Parents
      1. Mom
      2. Dad
    2. Siblings
      1. Brothers
        1. Some
        2. Random
        3. Name
      2. Sisters
        1. You
        2. Go
        3. Girls

Unordered Lists

These lists dont use numbers to form outlines.
(The kind of bullet is controlled using CSS.)

  • Good
  • Better
  • Best

They can also be multilayered:

  • Food
  • Drink
    • Non-Alcoholic
      • Juice
      • Shake
        • Strawberry
        • Protein
        • Banana
      • Milk
    • Alcoholic
      • Beer
      • Rum
      • Vodka
