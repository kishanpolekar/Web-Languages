A conversation with my dog

by Kishan Polekar

I enter the house

Good Evening Puppy!

What took you so long?

I close the door

I was at work. Like everyday...

Anyways, today was a fun day!

I was curious. What must have happened today?

I ran around the house in a cape. I'm BATDOG!

Where did you get the cape??

I pulled down the curtain from your room. Chill!

I was not chilled. I ran into my room to find the curtain perfectly fine and in order.

I'm confused. The curtain is up there! WHAT DID YOU DO WHEN I WAS GONE??

Nothing much. I like how you ran in all worried. Hehe. I just went to the neighbour's place and snatched their dining table cloth.

Haha. Very nice. Here, you get THIS for being a good boy!

The dog was overjoyed when I showed him a brand new tennis ball.
